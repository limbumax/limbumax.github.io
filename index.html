About Jane

I have been living and working in the UK now for almost eight years . I am originally from Nigeria. Being from an African background , i know all about the needs and requirements for Afro Carribean ladies and their hair and how to make them look good and natural. I am equally adept at making wigs for any race or culture and not just limited to Afro Carribean. I started making my own wigs when i first came to the UK , as I was struggling to find a nice natural looking wig to suit my particular look , so I decided to do it for myself .Now several years later , an idea that became a hobby has turned into a small business and I am now making and selling wigs on a regular basis ,and I also sell 100% Human Hair Bundles
